2. PRICING & TARIFFS All pricing is based on duty rates, freight rates, currency exchange rates, and applicable tariffs in effect at the time of quotation. Should any of these factors change — including but not limited to changes in US import tariffs, Section 301 duties, IEEPA executive tariffs, or ocean freight rates — IPS reserves the right to adjust pricing accordingly prior to or upon order confirmation. IPS will notify the buyer of any material changes before proceeding.

3. DELIVERY TERMS Delivery terms are as specified on this quotation. Duties, freight, and customs responsibilities are allocated between IPS and the buyer as described under the applicable Incoterm. All delivery timelines are estimates and are subject to manufacturing lead times, port conditions, and carrier availability.

4. ORDER CONFIRMATION This quotation does not constitute a binding agreement. A formal purchase order from the buyer and written acceptance from International Product Solutions, Inc. is required to confirm an order. Production lead times begin upon receipt of the confirmed deposit as specified in the payment terms above. IPS is not responsible for delays resulting from late deposit payments.

5. TOOLING & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY Any tooling, molds, dies, or fixtures developed by IPS on behalf of the buyer remain the property of IPS until full payment has been received. IPS will not share buyer designs with third parties without written consent.

6. ROLE OF IPS International Product Solutions, Inc. acts as a sourcing and supply chain management agent. IPS manages factory relationships, quality oversight, logistics, and import compliance on behalf of the buyer. Final product quality is governed by the product specification sheet and quality standards agreed upon in writing prior to production.
